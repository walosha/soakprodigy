About Consultant

SOAK Prodigy Professional Service (SPPS) is a value-added accounting solution provider for small and medium size firm across sectors, our service is targeted at providing tailor made accounting, bookkeeping, tax, CFO service and Accounting training solution for start-up, unstructured and structure firm by ensuring that their record are intact and readily available for scrutiny and filing of statutory returns to relevant authority at an optimum cost. We have team of committed and highly experience professionals with combined experience of over ten years in professional practice. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our client by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of our team.
